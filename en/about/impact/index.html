Mission

IMPaCT has the mission is to establish the pillars to facilitate the effective use of Precision Medicine in the Spanish Health System, ensuring scientific-technical quality, equity and efficiency in the use of available scientific resources to respond to the needs of the population. citizenship.

Vision

Become a reference scientific infrastructure for the development of Precision Medicine in Spain for safer, more efficient, preventive and predictive medicine.

Strategic Plan




IMPaCT is divided into three strategic axes, IMPaCT-Data corresponds to the strategic axis number 2, DATA SCIENCE. This axis is oriented towards the development and validation of an integration and joint analysis of clinical, molecular and genetic data, for secondary use of in a coordinated manner with strategic axes 1 and 3. Similarly, this axis will generate models that enable an efficient response to relevant questions for the SNS, promoting the generation of high-level knowledge based on these approaches.

Two transversal strategic lines have also been defined that correspond to ethics and scientific integrity and to internalization.

The detailed information about IMPaCT program is available in Instituto Carlos III (ISCIII) website
